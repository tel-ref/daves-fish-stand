The Build

One Plank at a Time

sawdust and all

No factory. No warehouse. Just a fishmonger's stand built by hand from weathered cedar, a heat press in the garage, and a phone on a tripod catching every step.

Why a fish stand

A proper monger's counter, for stickers

It started as a joke — what if vinyl stickers were sold like fresh fish, weighed out on ice, priced on a painted board, hollered about across a market? Then it stopped being a joke and became a stack of cedar in the garage.

I'm painting the signs, cutting the boards, wiring the little awning, and pressing each fish myself. When the stand's done it rolls out front when the flag's up, and loads into the truck for weekend markets. The whole messy, sawdust-covered build lives on TikTok, splinters and all.

Plank by Plank

The build, step by step

Every stage, in order. Drop your own TikTok stills into the slots as the clips go up.

01
Cedar
The boards

Found the cedar

Started with a stack of reclaimed fence boards — weathered grey, full of knots and old nail holes. Exactly the look I was after. Hauled them home in the truck and let them dry out in the garage.

TikTok still — unloading the cedar
02
Cuts
Sawdust season

Cut the counter

Two weekends of measuring twice and cutting once. The counter, the side rails, and the little shelf the price board hangs from. Sawdust in everything I own now.

TikTok still — the counter takes shape
03
Paint
By hand

Painted the signs

Hand-lettered the boards in vermillion and cream. “Honest Weights. Square Dealings.” Got the brush-script wrong twice before it sat right. Third time's the charm.

TikTok still — lettering the main sign
04
Awning
Stripes

Rigged the awning

Every good fish stall needs stripes. Sewed the vermillion-and-cream awning, then wired a string of warm bulbs underneath so it glows at dusk.

TikTok still — hanging the awning
05
Press
The good part

Fired up the press

The first real batch of fish, still warm off the platen. Weeded and trimmed each one by hand and laid them on the ice. This is the part I could do all day.

TikTok still — first fish off the press
06
Open
Day one

Packed the ice

Opening day. Flag up, awning out, counter full of glistening vinyl. Come find the stand — the rest of the story gets written at the market.
